Building on Ubuntu

The generic *n*x build instructions largely apply, with a few new points deserving attention.

Ubuntu packages PostgreSQL pieces separately

In addition to the postgresql-version and postgresql-server-dev-version packages, you may also need to separately install:

  • libecpg-dev
  • libkrb5-dev
